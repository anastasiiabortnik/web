Our Services

FAMILY MEDICINE

Comprehensive medical care for the whole family, from newborns to seniors. We provide preventive check-ups, vaccinations, routine screenings, and manage acute illnesses. Our team focuses on building long-term relationships to manage chronic diseases like diabetes and hypertension effectively.

INTERNAL MEDICINE

In-depth diagnosis and treatment of complex internal organ diseases in adults. Our internists (therapists) specialize in managing multiple chronic conditions simultaneously. We use advanced diagnostic methods to establish accurate diagnoses and develop comprehensive treatment plans for complex adult health issues.

CARDIOLOGY

Specialized care for your heart. Diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cardiovascular diseases. Our services include ECG, stress tests, Holter monitoring, and heart ultrasounds (Echocardiography). We also provide expert lifestyle counseling and management for hypertension and high cholesterol.

PHYSICAL THERAPY

Recovery after injuries, surgeries, or neurological events like strokes. We create individual rehabilitation programs including therapeutic exercises, manual therapy, and massage. Our goal is to restore mobility, reduce pain, and help you return to an active, independent life through personalized care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Family Medicine provides care for patients of all ages, from infants to the elderly. Internal Medicine (internists) focuses exclusively on adults (18+) and often specializes in diagnosing and managing complex medical conditions and chronic diseases in adult patients.

While many insurance plans require a referral from your primary care physician to see a specialist like a cardiologist, we also accept self-referrals. We recommend calling our office first to verify your insurance coverage and schedule a consultation.

Your first session involves a comprehensive assessment of your condition, pain level, and mobility. Based on this, your therapist will create a personalized treatment plan. Subsequent sessions typically include a combination of guided therapeutic exercises, manual (hands-on) therapy, and education on exercises you can do at home.
